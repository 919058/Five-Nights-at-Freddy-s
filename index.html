HOW TO PLAY

OBJECTIVE

Survive from 12:00 AM to 6:00 AM for 5 nights in a row at Freddy Fazbear's Pizza!

POWER & RESOURCES

  • Start with 100% power each night
  • Power decreases naturally
  • Closing doors uses power
  • When power hits 0%, it's game over

CONTROLS

  • SPACE - Toggle Camera View
  • D - Close Left Door
  • A - Open Left Door
  • K - Close Right Door
  • L - Open Right Door
  • ↑/↓ - Switch Cameras (in camera view)

ANIMATRONICS

Four animatronics patrol the pizzeria:

  • Freddy - Most dangerous
  • Bonnie - Aggressive
  • Chica - Moderate threat
  • Foxy - Unpredictable

STRATEGY

  • Use cameras to track animatronics
  • Close doors only when necessary
  • Each night gets progressively harder
  • Manage power carefully - don't waste it early
  • Listen for sounds indicating proximity
